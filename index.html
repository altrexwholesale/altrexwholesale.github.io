Company Overview

Altrex Wholesale Ltd is engaged in the wholesale procurement and distribution of electrical goods, electronic and telecommunications equipment, industrial machinery, and heavy manufacturing systems. The company purchases machinery and equipment from manufacturers and authorized suppliers and distributes them to multiple domestic and international locations on a wholesale and project-based basis.
